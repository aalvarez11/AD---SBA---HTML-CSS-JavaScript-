What You Can Do

Do you miss the days in the early 2000s where you could play flash games and feed a virtual pet?
This site was built with that nostalgia in mind, where you can do all of the following!

Pick from a selection of eggs to hatch a new pet that will be your own. Each pet has multiple values to care for such as: hunger, happiness and health.

Play from a regularly updated assortion of games where you can earn coins daily to spend on taking care of your pets!

Buy eggs, toys and food for your pets from a selection that changes daily.

Go head to head against other users in turn-based battles, taking advantage of elemental weaknesses to claim victory!

A single-player experience of battling, where users can fight monsters and claim goodies not found in the shop!
